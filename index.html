My Recipes

If life could only be as simple as following a recipe, we’d all be much happier. However, unlike food based recipes, life doesn’t provide you with standardized ingredients as each lives their unique life.

Salad

Mix together store-bought pesto and dijon mustard (about 50/50) in a bowl and spread generously over salmon filets. Bake on 350 degrees for 15 minutes (a little more if you like it more well done).

Vegetables

Cut 3/4 pounds of filet mignon into smaller squares. Also chop up 1 head of cauliflower, 3 large carrots, 1 yellow onion and a few cloves of garlic. Brown the meat in a pan and then remove.

Beans

Chop one yellow onion. Saute onion with a little olive oil until golden, and mix in 3/4 pounds of ground beef. As the beef is cooking, break it up with a spatula so that you have a very fine mixture.
